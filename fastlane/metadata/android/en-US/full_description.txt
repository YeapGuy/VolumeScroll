VolumeScroll is a lightweight Android utility that lets you scroll in apps using your volume buttons.

Key features:
- Scroll with volume up/down
- Quick Settings tile toggle
- Dual-volume-button chord toggle
- Adjustable scroll amount (70% to 120%)
- Optional invert direction
- Optional per-app allowlist (scroll only in selected apps)

How it works:
VolumeScroll uses an Accessibility Service to perform global scrolling actions across apps.

Privacy notes:
- No internet permission is required for core functionality.
- Accessibility access is only used for scrolling and key handling features.
